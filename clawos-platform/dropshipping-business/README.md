## 8. OPTIMIZATION & SCALING

### Conversion Rate Optimization

**Current Benchmarks:**
- Industry Average: 1-2%
- Target: 3-5%

**CRO Tactics:**
1. **Exit Intent Popup** - 10% discount capture
2. **Countdown Timer** - Limited-time offers
3. **Social Proof Notifications** - "Sarah from NYC just purchased"
4. **Sticky Add to Cart** - Mobile optimization
5. **One-Click Upsells** - Post-purchase offers
6. **Bundle Discounts** - "Buy 2 Get 1 Free"

### Email Marketing Automation

**Klaviyo Flows:**

1. **Welcome Series (5 emails)**
   - Email 1: Welcome + 10% off
   - Email 2: How to apply magnetic lashes (video)
   - Email 3: Customer testimonials
   - Email 4: FAQ/common concerns
   - Email 5: Limited-time offer reminder

2. **Abandoned Cart (3 emails)**
   - Email 1: 1 hour - "Forgot something?"
   - Email 2: 24 hours - "Still interested? + 10% off"
   - Email 3: 72 hours - "Last chance + free shipping"

3. **Post-Purchase (4 emails)**
   - Email 1: Order confirmation + usage tips
   - Email 2: 7 days - How are your lashes?
   - Email 3: 14 days - Request review + referral offer
   - Email 4: 30 days - Reorder reminder (lashes last 30 wears)

4. **Win-Back (3 emails)**
   - Trigger: 60 days no purchase
   - Incentive: 20% off comeback offer

### Scaling Strategy

**Phase 1: Testing (Weeks 1-2)**
- Budget: $50/day
- Test 3-5 audiences
- Test 3-5 creatives
- Kill anything under 2x ROAS

**Phase 2: Optimization (Weeks 3-4)**
- Increase budget on winners by 20% every 3 days
- Introduce lookalike audiences from purchasers
- Scale to $100-150/day

**Phase 3: Scale (Month 2+)**
- Budget: $300-500/day
- Horizontal scaling (duplicate winning ad sets)
- Expand to new countries
- Introduce new product variations

**Scaling Rules:**
- Never increase budget more than 20% per day
- Wait 3 days before scaling an ad set
- Maintain ROAS >3x while scaling
- Duplicate, don't edit winning ad sets
