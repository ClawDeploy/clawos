## CREATIVE TESTING FRAMEWORK

### Week 1 Testing Plan:
**Budget:** $50/day
**Test:** 3 angles √ó 2 creatives = 6 ad variations

| Ad Set | Creative | Copy Angle | Budget |
|--------|----------|------------|--------|
| 1 | Video - Problem/Solution | Convenience | $8/day |
| 2 | Video - Transformation | Confidence | $8/day |
| 3 | Image - Before/After | Cost Savings | $8/day |
| 4 | Image - Social Proof | Problem Agitation | $8/day |
| 5 | Carousel - How To | Educational | $8/day |
| 6 | Video - Testimonial | Social Proof | $10/day |

### Kill Criteria (Pause if):
- CTR < 1% after 3 days
- CPC > $2.00 after 3 days
- ROAS < 1.5 after 5 days
- Frequency > 3
- Negative feedback score high

### Scale Criteria (Increase budget):
- ROAS > 3x
- CTR > 2%
- CPA < $15
- Stable for 3+ days
