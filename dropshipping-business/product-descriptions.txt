## FAQ CONTENT (FOR SEO AND CONVERSIONS)

### Q: How long do magnetic lashes last?
A: With proper care, each pair of our magnetic lashes lasts 30+ wears. The magnetic eyeliner lasts approximately 2-3 months with daily use.

### Q: Are magnetic lashes safe?
A: Yes! Our magnetic lashes use cosmetic-grade magnets and FDA-approved eyeliner ingredients. They're actually gentler on natural lashes than traditional glue-on lashes.

### Q: Do magnetic lashes actually stay on?
A: Absolutely! Our 5-magnet technology creates a secure hold that lasts all day through work, workouts, and even swimming. The waterproof formula ensures they stay put.

### Q: Can I wear magnetic lashes if I have sensitive eyes?
A: Our magnetic lashes are ideal for sensitive eyes since they don't require irritating lash glue. However, if you have specific concerns, we recommend patch testing the eyeliner first.

### Q: How do I remove magnetic lashes?
A: Simply peel them off gently from the outer corner. Use makeup remover to clean any remaining eyeliner from your lids and the lash magnets.

### Q: What's the difference between magnetic lashes and regular false lashes?
A: Magnetic lashes attach to a magnetic eyeliner instead of glue. This means easier application, no mess, reusable lashes, and no damage to your natural lashes.
