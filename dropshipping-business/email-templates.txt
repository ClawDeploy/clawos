## EMAIL BEST PRACTICES

### Subject Line Tips:
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use emojis sparingly (1-2 max)
- Personalize with first name when possible
- Create curiosity or urgency
- A/B test everything!

### Email Design Tips:
- Single column layout (mobile-friendly)
- Large, tappable buttons
- Plenty of white space
- Clear hierarchy with headings
- Brand colors consistent
- Images optimized for fast loading

### Send Time Optimization:
- Tuesday-Thursday typically best
- 10am or 2pm local time
- Test different times for your audience
- Avoid Mondays and weekends (usually)

### Metrics to Track:
- Open Rate: Target 20%+
- Click Rate: Target 3%+
- Conversion Rate: Track per email
- Revenue per Recipient
- Unsubscribe Rate: Keep under 0.5%
